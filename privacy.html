Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Elevate Boxing respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Shipping address
  • Billing address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Order history
  • IP address
  • Browser and device information

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Process your orders
  • Deliver products
  • Provide customer support
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Improve our website
  • Prevent fraud
  • Send marketing emails (only if you choose to receive them)

4. Payments

All online payments are securely processed through Stripe. Elevate Boxing does not store your debit or credit card details.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, remember items in your shopping cart and help us understand how visitors use our website.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services including payment processors, analytics providers and email services. These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their services.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information against loss, misuse and unauthorised access. However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your Rights

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you and request corrections where appropriate, subject to Australian privacy laws.

9. Australian Privacy Law

Elevate Boxing complies with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) where applicable and is committed to handling personal information responsibly.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published on this page.
