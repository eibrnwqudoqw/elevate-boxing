Returns & Refunds

Last updated: July 2026

30-Day Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery, provided the item is unused, in its original condition and in its original packaging.

Faulty Products

If your item arrives damaged or develops a manufacturing fault, please contact us as soon as possible. We will work with you to arrange a replacement, repair or refund in accordance with Australian Consumer Law.

Change of Mind

Change-of-mind returns may be accepted at our discretion. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the product is faulty.

Refund Processing

Once your returned item has been received and inspected, approved refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Exclusions

  • Used products
  • Damaged items caused by misuse
  • Items returned outside the return period

Need Assistance?

Please contact us if you need help with a return.
